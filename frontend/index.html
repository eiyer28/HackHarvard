How It Works

Simple, secure, and seamless protection in four steps

1

Transaction Initiated

When you make a purchase, the merchant processes your credit card at a specific location (POS terminal).

2

Location Request

ProxyPay instantly requests your phone's current location through our secure mobile app.

3

Distance Validation

Our geospatial engine calculates the distance between your phone and the transaction location.

4

Approve or Deny

If locations match, transaction approved instantly. If not, it's blocked and you're notified immediately.
